02 · Infusion

Real fruit, infused. Then beets for the color.

Sukari starts as a clean, ultra-premium vodka. Whole fruit is infused in, and beets give it that natural rosy-coral hue you can see straight through the frosted glass.

The result reads as a fruity drink and pours as vodka, with no sugar carrying it and no burn following it. By most measures, it is one of the cleaner spirits you can pour.

  • Blood orange
  • Dragonfruit
  • Pineapple
  • Pear
  • Beet (the color)

04 · The Story

One person, her word, and a bottle she shaped herself.

Sukari was built by Rolesha “Ro” Brown, a service-disabled veteran and single mother, who started with nothing and documented every step. The fight for recognition is part of the brand, but the invitation is open to anyone who pours it.

The bottle is hers too. The curve and the cutout are deliberate, a quiet nod to a woman’s figure, which is also why the design earned an award of its own.

“The curves of the bottle, the cutout, that represents a woman’s curves.”

Rolesha Brown, founder
